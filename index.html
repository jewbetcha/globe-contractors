Globe Contractors, Inc.


Globe Contractors, Inc. is a family owned utility contractor that specializes in the installation
of sanitary sewer, storm sewer and water main systems.
Our corporate headquarters is loaced on 12+ acers in Pweaukee, Wisconsin.
Globe's area of operations includes the entire state of Wisconsin and the Upper Peninsula.
Our workforce consists of approximately 50 union employees.
