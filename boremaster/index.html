Bore Master

Globe’s sister company, Bore Master, has specialized in auger boring and pilot tube microtunneling since 1997, and has built a strong reputation in the up and coming trenchless technology sector. Get Bore Master on the job when installing utilities under rivers, railroads, buildings and highways. If you can’t dig it, we can drill it.

  • Guided boring
